Top three activities to do in Novi Sad

Explore Petrovaradin Fortress

Enjoy a peaceful climb during the day for the most stunning views of the city. Come back at night to see these old fortress rooms turn into the most lively dance clubs in Europe.

Meander the Party Scenes Along the Danube

Planning in advance can be so stressful. Instead, How about a carefree stroll along the Danube River with endless options in dining, humor, live music, and playing at the beach?
